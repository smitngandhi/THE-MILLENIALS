Hungry Snake

Hungry Snake is a sub-genre of action video games where the player maneuvers the end of a growing line, often themed as a snake. The player have to eat the food appearing on screen to grow the snake and must keep the snake from colliding with both other obstacles and itself, which gets harder as the snake lengthens.
